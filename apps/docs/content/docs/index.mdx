Sendero is a travel operator for humans and other agents.

- **For humans**, it is a persistent travel agent in WhatsApp, Slack, web, email, and the app.
- **For agents**, it is a tool and workflow surface: discover capabilities with `llms.txt`, call MCP tools, or delegate a full booking workflow.

## What you can build

- A consumer concierge that searches flights, books with Duffel, sends invoices, and stays with the traveler in-trip.
- A travel agency sub-agent on WhatsApp Business that sends prepaid escrow links under the agency brand.
- A corporate travel agent in Slack with policy checks, manager approvals, and CFO-grade spend reporting.
- An agent-to-agent travel rail where another LLM delegates search, hold, booking, settlement, and support to Sendero.
